Chicken Breast

Description

This recipe will show you how to prepare perfectly juicy chicken breasts with a crisp, golden brown exterior. No more dry, hard, and chewy chicken!

Let's begin.

Ingredients

  • Chicken Breasts (Divided) - x2
  • Salt - To Taste
  • Pepper - To Taste
  • Garlic Powder - To Taste
  • Vegetable Oil - 5-6 Tbsp

Steps

  1. Add 2-3 tbsp oil in a stainless steel pan, and put it on medium heat.
  2. Take 1 chicken breast and dry it out completely with a paper towel.
  3. Sprinkle few pinches of salt, black pepper, and garlic powder on both sides of the chicken breast.
  4. Once your pan is smoking slightly, lay down the chicken breast in it, with the smooth side facing down.
  5. Let it cook for 4-5 mins, until that side is crispy and golden brown.
  6. Then, give it a turn. Cook the other side for an additional 4-5 mins. Take it out once ready.
  7. Rest your cooked chicken breast on a wire-rack for 10 mins.
  8. Cut against the grain into pieces, and lay it out on your plate.
  9. Serve with a side of mashed potatoes, and roasted greens.
